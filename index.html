Why Choose us?

Featuring 3-star accommodation, Treebo Trend Grand Vistara Airport Suites is situated in Cochin, 18 km from Aster Medcity and 27 km from Kochi Biennale. The property is around 25 km from Cochin Shipyard, 15 km from Wonderla Kochi and 16 km from Kerala Museum. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk as well as free WiFi throughout the property.

Top Location

Situated just 700m from Cochin International airport and within a few hours from the top tourist attractions in Kerala, This is just the right place to stay.

Free Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi Access is available free of charge throughout the hotel premises so that you can browse in comfort!

Air Conditioning

All the rooms are fitted with air conditioning to give you max comfort no matter what the weather is outside.

24-hrs Availability

The hotel features a 24-hr front desk on call at any time.

Safe and Secure

The hotel is equipped with CCTV, fire extinguishers and smoke alarms. Family and Non-Smoking rooms are also available.

Standard Services

The rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kettle, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. At the hotel rooms come with a private bathroom and bed linen. Continental or Buffet breakfast available.
